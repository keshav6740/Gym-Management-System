FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers to common questions about our services and memberships

What are your membership options and pricing?

We offer three premium membership tiers: Basic Elite (₹1,000/month) with access to gym equipment and basic classes, Premium Elite (₹1,500/month) with additional group classes and nutrition guidance, and Platinum Elite (₹3,000/month) with personal training, 24/7 access, and premium amenities.

Do you offer personal training services?

Personal training is included with Platinum memberships and available as an add-on for other tiers. Our certified trainers provide customized workout plans, form correction, and one-on-one coaching to help you achieve your specific fitness goals.

Can I try a class or tour the facility before joining?

Yes! We offer complimentary trial classes and comprehensive facility tours. Contact us to schedule your visit and experience our premium fitness environment, meet our trainers, and see why FlexFusion Elite is the right choice for your fitness journey.

What safety measures and protocols do you have?

Safety is our top priority. We maintain the highest standards with regular equipment maintenance, certified trainers on-site, first aid stations, comprehensive insurance coverage, and strict hygiene protocols. All our trainers are CPR certified.

What parking and transportation options are available?

We're conveniently located in Canara Mall with ample free parking available. Members receive validated parking during workout sessions. The location is also accessible by public transport with bus stops nearby and easy highway access.
