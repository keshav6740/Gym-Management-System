Frequently Asked Questions

What are your membership options?

We offer three premium membership tiers: Basic Elite (₹1,000/month), Premium Elite (₹1,500/month), and Platinum Elite (₹3,000/month). Each tier includes different amenities and class access levels.

Do you offer personal training?

Yes! Personal training is included with Platinum memberships and available as an add-on for other tiers. Our certified trainers provide customized workout plans and one-on-one coaching.

Can I try a class before joining?

We offer complimentary trial classes and facility tours. Contact us to schedule your visit and experience our premium fitness environment firsthand.

What safety measures do you have in place?

We maintain the highest safety standards with regular equipment maintenance, certified trainers, first aid stations, and comprehensive insurance coverage for all members.

Do you have parking facilities?

Yes, we're located in Canara Mall with ample parking space available. Members receive validated parking during their workout sessions.
